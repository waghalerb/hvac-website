About Us

AVIRAT ENGINEERING TRAINING AND RESOURCES LIMITED is a team of HVAC professionals with over 20 years of experience in both HVAC training and system design. Our goal is to help you become an expert in the HVAC field by offering hands-on training and custom design solutions. Whether you're just starting or looking to upgrade your skills, we have the right program for you.

HVAC Training

We offer a range of HVAC training programs, from basic to advanced levels, designed to help you get certified and master HVAC installation, repair, and maintenance.

  • Beginner HVAC Training: Introduction to HVAC systems, tools, and basic maintenance. Duration: 4 weeks.
  • Advanced HVAC Training: System troubleshooting, energy efficiency, and complex installations. Duration: 6 weeks.
  • Online Certification: Flexible, on-demand learning with certification. Duration: Self-paced.
